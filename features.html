Features of Record.sh

Discover the powerful features that make Record.sh the ideal tool for recording and managing your terminal sessions.

Key Features

🖲

Effortless Recording

Record your terminal sessions with a single command. No complex setup required.

💾

Automatic Log Saving

Automatically save your terminal logs to a file, ensuring you never lose important data.

📝

Easy History Management

Access and manage your recorded logs easily, with simple commands for viewing and organizing.

💾

Customizable Settings

Configure your recording settings to fit your needs, including output format and log retention.

🗄

Secure Logs

Ensure the security of your logs with built-in features to control access and encryption.

📅

Timestamped Logs

Each log is timestamped for easy tracking and historical reference, making it easier to review sessions.
