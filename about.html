About Us

Learn more about the team behind Record.sh and our mission.

Our Mission

At Record.sh, our mission is to simplify the process of recording and managing terminal activity logs. We aim to provide a tool that is both powerful and easy to use, making it easier for developers, educators, and IT professionals to keep track of their work and share their terminal sessions with ease.

Our Story

Record.sh was created out of a need for a straightforward solution to log and review terminal sessions. Our project is driven by a dedicated individual who goes by the name "lelbois". While the project is spearheaded by lelbois, it benefits from the collective experience and insights gained from developing other successful ventures such as WebShell, NoteCDN, Dev.js, and more.

Meet the Team

Our team is small but highly skilled, with a focus on delivering high-quality tools for the tech community. The project reflects the passion and expertise of lelbois, who has been instrumental in driving innovation and providing valuable solutions through various other projects.
